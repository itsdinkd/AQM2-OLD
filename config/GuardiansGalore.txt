[Global] Config Version: 3

Particles: turns guardians' ambient particle effect on/off

Glow: used to adjust how bright a guardian appears (the strength of the glow effect)

Loudness: sets the volume of guardians' ambient noises

Health: sets the max health the the guardian

Speed: sets the max speed at which guardians travel

Attack: sets the amount of damage for guardian's melee attacks

Diet: sets the kinds of food the guardian will eat
